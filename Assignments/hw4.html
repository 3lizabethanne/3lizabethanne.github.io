Homework 4 Looping

Part I

In Part Ia, a FOR loop is created that iterates through numbers 5 through 21 and then displays the SUM and PRODUCT of every 4th integer for the user. In Part Ib, a WHILE loop iterates through numbers 3 through 21 and then displays the SUM and PRODUCT of every 3rd integer for the user.

Part II

In Part II, creates three tables using a for loop, each containing 10 rows. The contents of the tables calculate compound interest for 10 consecutive years using 3 different interest rates.
